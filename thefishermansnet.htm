THE FISHERMAN'S NET

I DREAM OF A FISHERMAN
HIS NET SUSPENDED
ABOVE THE WAVES
AS IT FALLS
THE BLUE SKY SLIPS THROUGH
THEN THE BLUE SEA
ALWAYS THE NET RETURNS
TETHERED BY A HEART STRING
TO THE SHORE
WITH EACH PATIENT THROW
HE FINDS PEACE WITH THE METHOD
