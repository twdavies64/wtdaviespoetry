Green and Blue

How deep the old forest dreams
An ancient dream that flows
from the heart of wood
through living water and
armored plates of bark

The rhythm of a thousand seasons
A silent pulse echoing
from wood to bone
and heart to heart
opening

The gate to
the green fields of the soul
