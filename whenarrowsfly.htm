When Arrows Fly

Does the winged spirit come
with wisdom, ignorance or death?
Have we passed through
this time
when arrows fly
unscathed?
I think never could it be!

In battles won or lost
will always be the bittersweet
And in the warrior’s heart
a tear for both the strong and weak 
for he or she having seen
glimpses from both sides
must walk the line
until the time
when arrows fly again
